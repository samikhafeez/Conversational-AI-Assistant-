TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE — TechFlow Support
=========================================

## Login Issues

### I can't log in — "Invalid credentials" error
1. Double-check your email address for typos.
2. Try resetting your password (see FAQ).
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again.
4. Disable any browser extensions (especially password managers that auto-fill incorrectly).
5. If using SSO (Google/Microsoft), ensure you're using the correct organisation account.

### My account is locked
Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts. Wait 30 minutes for the automatic unlock, or contact support@techflow.io with your account email to unlock it immediately.

### Two-factor authentication is not working
- Ensure the time on your phone is synchronised (use automatic time settings).
- If you've lost access to your authenticator app, use one of the backup codes provided during 2FA setup.
- If backup codes are unavailable, contact support with photo ID for account recovery.

## Performance Issues

### The application is running slowly
1. Check your internet connection speed (minimum 5 Mbps recommended).
2. Try a different browser to isolate browser-specific issues.
3. Disable browser extensions one by one to find conflicts.
4. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete on most browsers).
5. Check status.techflow.io for any ongoing incidents.

### File uploads are failing
- Maximum file size: 500 MB per file, 2 GB per upload session.
- Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PNG, JPG, MP4, ZIP.
- Ensure your storage quota is not full (check Settings > Storage).
- Try uploading over a wired connection rather than Wi-Fi.
- If the issue persists, try the TechFlow desktop app.

### Notifications are not arriving
1. Check your notification preferences under Settings > Notifications.
2. Ensure TechFlow emails are not filtered to spam.
3. Add noreply@techflow.io to your safe-sender list.
4. For Slack notifications, re-authorise the TechFlow Slack app.

## Data & Sync Issues

### My data is not syncing across devices
1. Log out and back in on the affected device to force a sync.
2. Check that you are connected to the internet.
3. Ensure you are using the same account on all devices.
4. Force-refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R / Cmd+Shift+R).

### I accidentally deleted a file
Files deleted within the last 30 days can be recovered from the Trash (left sidebar > Trash > Restore). Files older than 30 days are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

### Data export
Export all your data from Settings > Privacy > Export Data. You'll receive a .zip file via email within 2 hours containing all your files and metadata in standard formats.

---

## Integration Issues

### My Slack integration stopped working
1. Go to Settings > Integrations > Slack and click Reconnect.
2. Re-authorise TechFlow in your Slack workspace settings.
3. Check that the TechFlow Slack app is not restricted by your workspace admin.

### API returning 401 Unauthorised
- Your API key may have expired. Regenerate it from Settings > Developer > API Keys.
- Ensure you're passing the key in the Authorization header: `Bearer YOUR_API_KEY`.
- Check that your IP is not blocked in the API allowlist (Enterprise feature).

### Zapier automation is not triggering
1. Verify the trigger conditions in your Zap are correct.
2. Re-authenticate TechFlow inside Zapier.
3. Check Zapier's task history for specific error messages.
4. Ensure your TechFlow account has API access (Professional plan or above).
